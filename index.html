Game Theory

  • Computed the Nash equilibrium of dozens games including: Pai Gow Tiles, Pia Gow Poker, twenty versions of CA blackjack, Booray, and several heads up variations of poker.
  • We won a Gold Medal for the most innovative gaming product at the American Gaming Summit in Las Vegas.
  • Devised a game theoretic algorithm for competing slot machine teams. The algorithm was successfully employed by a large, successful slot team.
  • Developed many ways to exploit inefficiencies in non-random shuffling procedures. See “How the Pro’s Win Millions” an Esquire cover story on our blackjack system in play.
  • Developed and patented optimal poker equations. Solving final round equilibrium for equal and skewed distribution contests.

Computer Vision

  • Designed the BST Scan IOS application that allows an anthropologist to collect and record data about a child with simple touch based input.
  • As data is entered, it is automatically synced to the cloud or can be uploaded at a later date.
  • It there are any abnormalities in the measurements, the measurer is notified.
  • The measurer can capture 2D (photograph) and 3D captures of the patient at various positions.
  • The 3D scans are taken in quick bursts so an impatient child can be successfully modeled.
