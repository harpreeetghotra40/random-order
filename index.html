Computer Vision

  • Designed the BST Scan IOS application that allows an anthropologist to collect and record data about a child with simple touch based input.
  • As data is entered, it is automatically synced to the cloud or can be uploaded at a later date.
  • It there are any abnormalities in the measurements, the measurer is notified.
  • The measurer can capture 2D (photograph) and 3D captures of the patient at various positions.
  • The 3D scans are taken in quick bursts so an impatient child can be successfully modeled.
